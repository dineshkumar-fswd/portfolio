My Journey

My journey into web development began with curiosity and soon evolved into a purpose. I started with basic static websites using HTML and CSS, moved on to interactive designs with JavaScript, and later explored dynamic website creation using WordPress and WooCommerce.

I discovered my passion for web development in my second year of college, which led me to internships and real-world projects. In my final year, I was entrusted with a live WordPress project, where I independently managed design, development, security, and maintenance—an experience that strengthened my responsibility and technical confidence. One of my key projects, Ashwinis Herbs and Foods, received excellent feedback from the client and led to another opportunity to build a static corporate website, Rashmivat Edutech. These projects have shaped me into a problem solver who can deliver practical, real-world solutions.

Currently, I am expanding my skills with Python, Django, and MySQL to become a well-rounded full-stack developer, while continuing to learn and improve every day.
